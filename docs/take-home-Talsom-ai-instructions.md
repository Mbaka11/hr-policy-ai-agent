# Take-home: Design and Development of an AI Agent for HR Policy Q&A

## Overview

As part of the selection process for the **AI Internship at Talsom**, candidates are required to design and develop a prototype AI agent capable of answering questions related to internal HR policies.

This project aims to evaluate:

- Your technical reasoning
- Your architectural decisions
- Your ability to structure an AI solution end-to-end

---

## Context

The HR department of a professional services company (200 employees) aims to deploy an internal AI agent to answer frequently asked questions about internal policies, such as:

- Vacation policies
- Employee benefits
- Training programs
- Performance evaluation processes

Currently, the information is scattered across:

- SharePoint intranet
- PDF documents
- HR emails

---

## Objective

Develop an AI agent using a **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** architecture capable of answering HR policy-related questions accurately and reliably.

---

## Guidelines

- You are free to choose the approach, tools, and technologies.
- You are allowed to use an LLM to assist you throughout the process (including code generation), **except for the video recording**.
- You must source your own HR policy data from publicly available resources.
- Creativity is encouraged — the goal is to understand your reasoning and design approach.

## Expected Deliverables

### Deliverable #1 — Technical Document

A technical document (free format, can be included in the GitHub repository) covering:

#### 1. Agent Architecture

Describe the proposed architecture:

- Language model used
- Information retrieval approach (RAG, knowledge base, etc.)
- Tools, frameworks, or connectors

Include a **flow diagram** illustrating how a user query is processed.

#### 2. System Instructions

Provide the **system prompt** of the agent, including:

- Behavioral rules
- Tone
- Limitations
- Conditions under which the agent should escalate to a human

#### 3. Edge Case Handling

Explain how your system handles:

- Out-of-scope questions
- Contradictory information across sources
- Sensitive requests (e.g., harassment, sick leave)
- Inappropriate or unsafe queries

#### 4. Evaluation Strategy

Describe how you would evaluate the quality of the agent before deployment.

Provide **3–5 concrete test cases**.

#### 5. Source Code

Provide the full implementation in a **public GitHub repository**.

---

### Deliverable #2 — Video Presentation

A **5–10 minute video** explaining:

- Your solution
- Your technical choices
- Limitations and potential improvements

#### Requirements:

- The **first minute must be in English** (brief self-introduction)
- The rest of the video must be in **French** (technical terms in English are acceptable)

#### Submission:

- You may use **WeTransfer** to send the video.
