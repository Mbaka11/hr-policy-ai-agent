# Employee Benefits

McGill University offers a comprehensive benefits package designed to support employee well-being and financial security.

## Core Benefits

- Health and dental insurance
- Pension plan
- Life insurance
- Short-term and long-term disability coverage
- Paid leave and vacation
- Tuition assistance
- Employee and Family Assistance Program (EFAP)

---

## Health and Dental Plans

The benefits package includes coverage for:

- Medical services not covered by provincial healthcare
- Prescription drugs
- Hospital accommodations
- Dental services (preventive, restorative, orthodontic)

### Key Features

- Coverage applies to employees and eligible dependents
- Some plans are mandatory unless alternative coverage is provided
- Out-of-pocket maximum applies depending on coverage type

---

## Insurance

### Life Insurance

- Basic life coverage: approximately equal to employee salary
- Optional coverage available up to higher limits

### Disability Coverage

- Provides income protection in case of illness or injury
- Includes short-term and long-term disability options

---

## Pension Plan

- Employees and employer both contribute
- Contribution rates vary based on age and salary
- Designed for long-term retirement savings

---

## Additional Programs

### Employee Assistance Program (EFAP)

Provides support for:

- Mental health
- Personal issues
- Family-related concerns

---

### Tuition Assistance

Employees may receive financial support for educational development.

---

## Notes

- Benefits eligibility depends on employment status (full-time, part-time, etc.)
- Coverage may change based on life events (marriage, retirement, etc.)
