# Health and Dental Benefits

## Overview

This section provides information about **supplemental health and dental coverage**, including:

- Coverage details (what is covered and not covered)
- Reimbursement percentages
- Annual out-of-pocket maximums
- Eligibility rules

---

## Insurance Provider

- **Provider:** Manulife Financial
- **Policy Number:** 85210
- **Customer Service:** 1-800-268-6195

---

## Benefits Card

Employees and eligible dependents can access a personalized benefits card through **Manulife's Plan Member Secure Site**.

### Access Instructions

1. Visit the Plan Member Secure Site
2. Select **Login/Register**
3. If not registered:
   - Click **Registration**
   - Follow instructions on the Manulife website

---

### Usage

- Present the benefits card to:
  - Pharmacists
  - Dentists
  - Health service providers
- Claims are submitted electronically to Manulife
- Covered amounts are reimbursed automatically
- No paper claim submission is required

---

### Card Information

The benefits card includes:

- Group plan number (85210)
- Employee certificate number
- Manulife customer service contact

The reverse side includes:

- Emergency contact numbers
- Travel assistance plan number

## Family Coverage and Eligible Dependents

### Legal Requirement

Under Quebec law, employees and eligible family members under age 65 must enroll in the Supplemental Health Plan if no alternative group drug coverage is available.

---

## Definition of Dependent

A dependent is defined as:

- An employee’s **Spouse** or **Child**
- Must be covered under the provincial plan
- Must reside in Canada

---

## Eligibility Rules

### Children

A child is eligible if they:

- Are unmarried
- Are financially dependent on the employee
- Are not employed full-time on a permanent basis
- Are not eligible for another group plan

And meet one of the following conditions:

- Under **21 years old**, OR
- Under **26 years old** if a full-time student

---

### Special Case: Disabled Dependents

A child may remain eligible beyond standard age limits if:

- They have a **mental or physical disability**
- They are unable to engage in gainful employment
- They depend on the employee for care and support

#### Requirements:

- Proof must be submitted within **31 days after age 21**
- Medical documentation required
- Reviewed by Manulife

---

### Stepchildren

- Must live with the employee to be eligible

---

### Spouse

A spouse is defined as:

- A legal spouse, OR
- A person living with the employee in a marriage-like relationship for at least one year

#### Notes:

- Only **one spouse** is eligible under the plan
- If multiple qualify, the most recently qualified individual is considered the eligible spouse

---

## Notes

- Eligibility and coverage depend on employment status
- Additional rules may apply based on provincial regulations
