# Life Insurance Plan

## Overview

The life insurance benefit provides a financial payment to a **designated beneficiary** in the event of the employee’s death.

---

## Coverage

### Basic Life Insurance

- Coverage amount:
  - **1× employee’s annual salary**

---

### Optional Life Insurance

- Employees may purchase additional coverage:
  - Up to **$1,000,000**

---

## Insurance Provider

- **Provider:** Industrial Alliance
- **Policy Number:** 98500

---

## Notes

- Employees can designate a beneficiary to receive the benefit
- Optional coverage allows increased financial protection beyond the basic plan
