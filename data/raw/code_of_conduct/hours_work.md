# Hours of Work Policy

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- Regular non-unionized, non-academic staff
- Excludes University Executives (“E”)

---

## Standard Work Hours

### Management (M) and Clerical Staff (C)

- Workweek: **33.75 hours**
- Workdays: Monday to Friday
- Daily hours: **6.75 hours**
- Includes:
  - **1.25-hour unpaid lunch break**

- Schedule:
  - Determined by department/unit

---

### Technical and Library Assistant Staff

- Workweek: **35 hours**
- Workdays: Monday to Friday
- Daily hours: **7 hours**
- Includes:
  - **1-hour unpaid lunch break**

- Schedule:
  - Determined by department/unit

---

## Flexible Work Arrangements

- Flexible schedules may be allowed
- Requires approval from:
  - Dean or Senior Administrative Head
  - Human Resources

---

## Summer Fridays

### Overview

- Employees may receive **Fridays off during summer**
- Applies between:
  - **June 24 (Fête Nationale)** and **Labour Day**

- Based on:
  - Agreement with Université de Montréal
  - 2.8% reduction in work hours instead of salary increase

---

### Vacation Interaction

- If a vacation includes a Friday:
  - That Friday **does NOT count as a vacation day**

---

### Working on Summer Fridays

#### Morning Work

- Compensation:
  - Time off OR
  - Straight-time pay

---

#### Afternoon Work

- Compensation:
  - Time off OR
  - **Time-and-a-half overtime pay**

---

### Compensatory Time

- Must be:
  - Agreed with supervisor
- When possible:
  - 3-day weekend should be preserved

---

## Special Case (Pre-2006 Employees)

Employees hired before **December 1, 2006**:

- Receive **half-day credit** if they miss Summer Friday mornings due to:
  - Short-term disability
  - Maternity leave

- Credit is:
  - Given as compensatory time upon return

---

## Notes

- Work schedules are department-specific
- Summer Friday schedules are announced annually
- Compensation rules depend on timing of work (morning vs afternoon)
