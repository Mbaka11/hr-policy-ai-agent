# Rest Periods Policy

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- Regular non-unionized administrative and support staff
- Excludes University Executives (“E”)

---

## Rest Period Entitlement

- Employees are entitled to:
  - **1 rest period of 15 minutes**
  - Per **complete half-day of work**

- Compensation:
  - Rest periods are **paid**
  - No loss of salary

---

## Definition of Half-Day

- A "half-day of work" is:
  - **Half of a regular workday**

- A "regular workday" is defined by:
  - The **Hours of Work Policy**
  - Based on the employee’s role

---

## Scheduling Rules

- Timing and scheduling are determined by:
  - Department or faculty management

---

## Restrictions

- Rest periods:
  - **Cannot be accumulated**
  - **Cannot be used to extend**:
    - Vacation
    - Holidays
    - Other paid leave

---

## Notes

- Rest periods must be taken within the workday
- Policy revised March 2004
