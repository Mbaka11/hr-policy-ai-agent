# Organizational Development Workshops at McGill University

## Overview

Organizational Development (OD) offers a broad catalogue of **interactive, practical short workshops** to help employees and teams improve performance, collaboration, communication, and well-being. These workshops are particularly suited to teams and managers with specific or short-term training needs.

Availability varies by semester. Employees should consult the live event calendar to confirm scheduled sessions:  
https://www.mcgill.ca/myhealthyworkplace/events/calendar

In addition to OD workshops, **My Healthy Workplace** partner organizations offer supplementary workshops through the same calendar.

---

## Workshop Catalogue

The following workshops are offered by Organizational Development (subject to scheduling each term):

### Leadership & Management

- **Situational Leadership** — Adapting leadership style to the needs and readiness of each employee
- **McGill's Performance Dialogue** — Practical workshop on conducting effective Performance Dialogue sessions
- **Coaching for Employee Engagement** — Skills for supervisors to use coaching approaches to improve engagement
- **Leading High Performing Teams in a Hybrid World** — Strategies for team effectiveness across hybrid settings
- **Leading your Team with Psychological Safety** — Building environments where employees feel safe to speak up
- **Managing in a Unionized Environment** — Understanding the union-management framework at McGill
- **Courageous Conversations** — Frameworks for addressing difficult topics constructively
- **Disciplinary and Administrative (Non-Disciplinary) Measures** — Legal and procedural aspects of progressive discipline

### Change & Strategy

- **Change Management** — Understanding and applying change management principles
- **Effectively Leading and Managing Change** — Practical tools for guiding teams through organizational transitions
- **Surviving & Thriving in Times Of Change** — Personal resilience and adaptability during change
- **Environmental Scan & S.W.O.T. Analysis** — Strategic analysis frameworks for teams and units
- **Planning Strategies and Actions** — Translating goals into concrete action plans
- **Rethinking the Way We Measure Success** — Reframing performance metrics beyond traditional KPIs
- **Envisioning Sustainability at McGill** — Incorporating sustainability thinking into unit planning

### Communication & Interpersonal Skills

- **Boost Your Emotional Intelligence** — Building self-awareness and empathy in professional relationships
- **Listening & Effective Communication Across Differences** — Communication skills for diverse and inclusive workplaces
- **Put Your Best Email Forward** — Writing clear, professional, and efficient workplace emails
- **Identify Your MBTI Preference** — Myers-Briggs Type Indicator for self-awareness and team dynamics
- **Group Dynamics & Facilitation** — Facilitating effective group discussions and meetings
- **Working With Intergenerational Teams** — Bridging generational differences in the workplace

### Wellbeing & Mental Health

- **The Working Mind** — Mental health awareness and stigma reduction in the workplace
- **Mental Health First Aid (2-day certification training)** — Certification for responding to mental health crises
- **Managing Stress** — Practical strategies for managing workplace and personal stress
- **Universal and Empowering Tools for Transitions** — Support tools for navigating personal and professional transitions
- **Managing Your Career** — Career planning and self-advocacy strategies

### Hybrid & Remote Work

- **IT Skills for a Hybrid Workplace** — Digital tools and skills for effective hybrid collaboration
- **Ergonomics Onsite, Offsite, and In-Between** — Setting up safe and healthy work environments across settings
- **Team Work Distribution in Hybrid Workplaces** — Fairly distributing work across hybrid team members
- **Work Distribution in Hybrid Workplaces** — (individual-focused session on the same topic)
- **LiL Course Club: Be an Effective Hybrid or Virtual Employee** — LinkedIn Learning group discussion series on hybrid effectiveness
- **LiL Course Club: Develop and Promote your Hybrid Competence** — LinkedIn Learning discussion series
- **LiL Course Club: Leveraging Virtual and Hybrid Teams for Improved Effectiveness** — LinkedIn Learning discussion series

### Project & Process Management

- **Project Management I** — Introduction to project management methodologies and tools
- **Project Management II** — Advanced project management for complex, multi-stakeholder projects
- **LEAN Six Sigma White Belt** — Introduction to LEAN and Six Sigma process improvement principles
- **Managing Conflict** — Practical conflict resolution frameworks (multiple sections available)

### Recognition & Workplace Culture

- **Recognition and Appreciation in the Workplace** — Building cultures of recognition and understanding its impact on engagement
- **Service Excellence** — Delivering high-quality, client-centred service in a university context
