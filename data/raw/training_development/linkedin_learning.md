# LinkedIn Learning at McGill University

## What is LinkedIn Learning?

LinkedIn Learning is an **online learning platform** that provides access to thousands of on-demand video courses across topics including technology, business, leadership, data analysis, project management, and creative skills. McGill has purchased institution-wide access and made it available to all eligible staff.

LinkedIn Learning is separate from LinkedIn (the professional networking site). You do **not** need a LinkedIn account to access LinkedIn Learning — it is accessed independently via McGill's Single Sign-On.

## Who is Eligible?

LinkedIn Learning is available to all **Administrative and Support Staff** at McGill University.

## How to Access

1. Visit the Single Sign-On portal: http://lnkd.in/eKvf67m
2. Sign in with your **McGill credentials** (same username and password used for Workday, Minerva, etc.)
3. You will be taken directly to the LinkedIn Learning platform — no personal LinkedIn account needed

## LinkedIn Learning Workshops & Learning Paths

Organizational Development has curated **structured Learning Paths** — sequences of LinkedIn Learning courses aligned to McGill's development priorities and Behavioural Competencies. These paths are also featured in OD's workshop calendar as "LiL Course Club" sessions, where staff discuss LinkedIn Learning content together in structured peer groups.

Current LiL Course Club themes include:

- Be an Effective Hybrid or Virtual Employee
- Develop and Promote Your Hybrid Competence
- Leveraging Virtual and Hybrid Teams for Improved Effectiveness

More information on curated Learning Paths:  
https://www.mcgill.ca/od/linkedin-learning/linkedin-learning-workshops-learning-paths

## Why LinkedIn Learning?

This service was introduced in response to the growing needs of McGill's hybrid and virtual work environment. It allows staff to:

- Learn at their own pace, on their own schedule
- Access content on both personal and professional development
- Build skills aligned to their Performance Dialogue development plans
- Stay current with evolving technical tools and professional practices
