Dear Colleague,

The annual **Benefits Open Enrollment period is now open** from **October 15 to October 31, 2025**. This is your opportunity to review and update your benefits coverage for the calendar year beginning January 1, 2026.

**This is the only time of year you may change your coverage levels outside of a qualifying life event (marriage, birth/adoption of a child, divorce, spouse losing coverage, etc.).**

## What You Can Change During Open Enrollment

- Health Plan coverage level (Employee only / Employee + Spouse / Employee + Children / Family)
- Dental Plan coverage level
- Opt out of coverage if you have duplicate coverage through a spouse's plan (coordination of benefits)
- Re-enroll if you previously opted out
- Update beneficiary designations for Life Insurance

## What's New for 2026

**1. Expanded Paramedical Coverage**
The Supplemental Health Plan now covers **registered psychologists and registered social workers** at 80%, up to **$1,500 per year** (previously $800). This applies to all coverage tiers.

**2. Virtual Healthcare (Dialogue App)**
McGill has renewed its contract with Dialogue for virtual primary care and mental health services. All benefits-eligible employees and their covered dependents have access at no additional premium cost.
Access: https://www.dialogue.co (use your McGill employee ID to register)

**3. RAMQ Coordination Update**
Effective January 1, 2026, the coordination-of-benefits rules with RAMQ are updated to comply with the revised Régie de l'assurance maladie du Québec regulations. Employees who have resided in Quebec for less than 3 months remain ineligible for RAMQ and should contact benefits.hr@mcgill.ca to ensure continuous coverage.

## How to Make Changes

1. Log into **Workday** at http://workday.mcgill.ca
2. Navigate to **Benefits** → **Open Enrollment**
3. Review your current elections and make any desired changes
4. Click **Submit** before October 31 at 11:59 PM EST

**If you do not act**, your current 2025 elections will automatically carry over to 2026, with updated premium rates applied.
