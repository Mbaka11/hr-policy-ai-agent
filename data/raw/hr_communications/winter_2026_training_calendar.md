## Programs with Open Enrollment This Semester

**Leadership Development Program (LDP) – 2026 Cohort**
Applications are now open for the 2026 LDP cohort, which begins in May 2026. The LDP is a **1-year program** for management-level staff (MCM grade and above) focused on strategic leadership, self-awareness, and institutional effectiveness. Cohort size is limited to 18 participants.
Application deadline: **February 28, 2026**
Information session: February 4, 2026 at 12:00 PM (virtual)
Apply: https://www.mcgill.ca/od/programs

**LEAN Six Sigma – Green Belt (New Cohort)**
Following the success of the White Belt program, OD is launching its first **in-house Green Belt cohort** in partnership with an external facilitator. This 6-session program spans March through June 2026.
Prerequisite: LEAN Six Sigma White Belt or equivalent
Max 10 participants | Application deadline: February 13, 2026

## How to Register

All registrations are handled through:
https://www.mcgill.ca/od/register

- Workshops are offered free of charge to all eligible McGill staff
- Your manager's approval may be required by your unit — check with your supervisor
- Cancellations must be made at least **48 hours in advance** to free up space for colleagues on the waitlist
- **Attendance tracking:** Completion is recorded in your Minerva training record. Extract your training history at: https://www.mcgill.ca/od/how-extract-your-training-record-minerva

Do not miss this opportunity to invest in your development. We look forward to seeing you!
