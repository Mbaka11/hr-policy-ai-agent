Dear Colleagues,

We are pleased to announce that the **Performance Dialogue (PD) cycle for 2026 is now open**.

The Performance Dialogue is McGill's framework for structured, documented conversations between employees and their supervisors. It is an opportunity to reflect on the past year, set objectives for the year ahead, and plan your professional development.

## Key Dates

| Milestone                                              | Deadline       |
| ------------------------------------------------------ | -------------- |
| Employee self-assessment forms submitted to supervisor | March 6, 2026  |
| PD sessions completed                                  | April 18, 2026 |
| Signed forms submitted to HR                           | April 30, 2026 |

**Please note:** These deadlines apply to all Management (M), Confidential (C), and MPEX staff. Unionized staff timelines may differ — please consult your collective agreement or contact your HR Advisor.

## Reference Period

The 2026 Performance Dialogue covers the period from **January 1, 2025 to December 31, 2025**.

## What's New in 2026

- **Updated Behavioural Competencies Grid** — The 2026 grid reflects revised proficiency expectations for M3 and M4 grades in the IST (Information Systems and Technology) job family. If you are in an IST role, please download the new grid before beginning your assessment.
- **Sustainability Objectives** — For the second consecutive year, OD is encouraging units to incorporate at least one sustainability-related SMART objective. A new "Incorporating Sustainability in the PD" guide is available.
- **Workload Management Guide** — New this year: a 5-step structured guide to raise workload imbalance within the PD conversation rather than as a separate process.

## How to Get Started

1. Download the **Performance Dialogue and Appraisal Form – 2026** from the OD website.
2. Complete your self-assessment sections (Individual Work Objectives, Behavioural Competencies, Training & Development, Career Aspirations).
3. Send the completed draft to your supervisor **at least one week before your scheduled PD session.**
4. Meet with your supervisor for the Performance Dialogue conversation.
5. Both parties sign the form. Supervisor submits to HR.
