Dear Colleagues,

This is a friendly but important reminder that the **current vacation reference year closes on May 31, 2026**.

Under McGill's Vacation Policy, the reference year runs from **June 1 to May 31**. Vacation credits earned during this period **must be used before the reference year ends**. Credits that remain unused at May 31 are **not carried over** to the next year and are not paid out (except in specific circumstances such as termination of employment).

---

## Why This Matters

Historically, approximately **12% of eligible staff carry unused vacation balances past May 31**, resulting in forfeiture of earned time off. This reminder is sent annually to help you avoid losing vacation credits you have already earned.

---

## Check Your Current Balance

You can view your remaining vacation balance at any time in **Workday**:

1. Log in at http://workday.mcgill.ca
2. Navigate to **Time Off** → **Time Off Balance**
3. Your balance will show accrued credits and used days for the current reference year

If your balance appears incorrect, contact your HR Advisor or payroll directly.

---

## Scheduling Your Vacation

While you are entitled to take your vacation, it must be **scheduled with your supervisor's approval** and taken at a mutually agreed-upon time that does not unduly disrupt operations. The University's operational needs are a legitimate factor in scheduling.

**Tips for scheduling before May 31:**

- Submit vacation requests as early as possible — your supervisor needs reasonable lead time
- Consider partial days or long weekends if a block of full weeks is not possible
- If operational constraints genuinely prevent you from taking your vacation before May 31, discuss with your supervisor and contact HR — exceptional carryover arrangements may be possible on a case-by-case basis

---

## Special Situations

**On Leave (Maternity/Parental/Sick Leave):** Vacation continues to accrue during paid leaves. If you are on leave and cannot take vacation before May 31, contact hr.advisory@mcgill.ca — your situation will be assessed individually.

**Termination of Employment:** Upon termination, any unused vacation credits as of the termination date are paid out as a lump sum based on your daily rate.

**New Employees:** If you joined McGill after June 1, 2025, your vacation entitlement for this reference year is prorated based on your start date.
