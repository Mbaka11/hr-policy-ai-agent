# Spouse Leave Policy (Birth or Adoption)

## Entitlement

An employee may take **5 days of paid leave** at the time of:

- Birth of their child
- Adoption of a child
- Termination of pregnancy starting from the **20th week of pregnancy**

An employee who adopts the child of their spouse is also entitled to this leave.

---

## Rules

- The employee must inform the University of their absence **as soon as possible**.
- The leave can be **split into individual days** at the employee's request.
- Must be taken **within 15 days** of the child's arrival at either parent's home, or within 15 days of the termination of pregnancy.
