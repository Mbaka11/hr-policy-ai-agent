# Marriage and Civil Union Leave Policy

## Scope

This policy applies to all regular non-unionized administrative and support staff, with the exception of University Executives ("E").

---

## Entitlements

- An employee is entitled to **five (5) working days of paid leave** for their own marriage or civil union.
- The employee may extend this with unpaid leave, accumulated vacation, and/or accumulated overtime, **not exceeding fifteen (15) working days** in total.
- An employee is entitled to **one (1) day of paid leave** on the day of the wedding or civil union of their: father, mother, son, daughter, brother, sister, or child of their spouse.

---

## Procedure

The employee must advise the University of their absence as soon as possible. Social leaves will not be granted if they coincide with vacation or another University leave policy.
