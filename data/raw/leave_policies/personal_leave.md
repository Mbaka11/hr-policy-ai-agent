# Personal Leave Policy

## Scope

This policy applies to all regular non-unionized non-academic staff, with the exception of University Executives ("E"). "Regular" refers to all staff in Excluded positions and managerial staff.

---

## Policy

- An employee who is required to be absent for a personal reason not covered by any other University leave policy may be granted **paid leave of up to two (2) working days per financial year**, without loss of salary or rights.
- Personal leave applies to specific incidents (foreseen or unforeseen) requiring the employee's presence, such as: illness of a spouse or dependent, legal affairs, etc.
- Personal leave **may not be used as vacation**, nor as an extension of vacation or any other University leave, with the exception of Social Leaves.
- Personal leave must be taken in periods of **not less than one-half (1/2) day**.
- For predictable events, the employee must advise their immediate supervisor **at least two (2) days in advance**.
