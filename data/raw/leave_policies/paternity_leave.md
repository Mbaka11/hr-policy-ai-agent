# Paternity Leave Policy

## Overview

Paternity leave is **in addition to** the 5-day paid spouse leave (see Spouse Leave policy) and the 65-week parental leave.

## Scope

This policy applies to all regular non-unionized non-academic staff, with the exception of University Executives ("E"). Temporary employees are also covered.

---

## Procedures

- Employees are entitled to up to **5 uninterrupted weeks without pay**, starting no earlier than the week of the child's birth and ending no later than **78 weeks after birth**.
- May be split into weeks at the employee's request with employer agreement.
- Employees receiving QPIP indemnities receive **95% of regular salary** (minus QPIP benefits and normal deductions) for up to 5 weeks.
- All benefits continue during paternity leave.
- Request must be submitted via Workday with at least **3 weeks' notice** to the supervisor, indicating start date and return date. Notice may be shorter if the child arrives before the expected birth date.
- The leave **cannot be transferred to or shared with the other parent**.
- The leave cannot be interrupted without University authorization or in specific cases mentioned in the Act.
